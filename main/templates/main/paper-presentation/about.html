Paper Presentation

The Paper Presentation event, held during APOGEE is a competition where participants get an opportunity to exhibit the results of their investigations or researches. This event has been an integral part of APOGEE since its inception in 1983 and has seen papers from diverse backgrounds of science, engineering, humanities and management being presented and evaluated.

The event aims to promote research among undergraduates and uphold the primary motive of our institute: “train young men and women able and eager to create and put into action such ideas, methods, techniques and information.”

Rules of the Event

A particular paper can be presented only in one category. Also, participants can present more than one paper. However, the abstracts should be submitted separately.

Only genuine and original paper abstracts should be uploaded. Abstracts found copied from any source like internet/ journals etc. will be disqualified. Plagiarized content will be strictly dealt with and would lead to participant's disqualification. In case of any discrepancy, organizers decision will be final and binding.

Round One

  • Participants have to upload their abstracts for screening in the appropriate format using the online portal.
  • The prescribed format (hyperlink prescribed format) must be followed failing which may lead to disqualification of the participants. The deadline for abstract submission is 8th November 2018,11:59 PM.
  • The participants will receive comprehensive feedbacks about their abstracts and suggestions for improvement from a panel of distinguished professors of the concerned field.

Round Two

  • Participants are supposed to submit their papers along with their abstracts in this round.
  • The submission of the same should be done using the online portal.
  • The prescribed format for them can be found here.
  • The papers will go through an intensive plagiarism check, failing which the paper will be disqualified.
  • The participants will be able to view the status of their abstracts and papers through the online portal.

Round Three

  • This is the final round of the event and will be held during APOGEE 2019 (28th-31st March, 2019) at BITS Pilani.
  • Shortlisted students are expected to present their work on-stage using PowerPoint in front of a panel of judges.
  • Maximum Time Limit for the same is 8 minutes, which will be followed by a question answering session by the panel. Once all the participants have concluded their presentation, the judges will announce the winner.
